CLIENTS

Do clients need a subscription to use the app?

Clients can register on the app free of charge. They can find barbers, hairdressers, nail techs, makeup artists and more near them with no subscription. A 5% commission is applied on bookings.

How do I book a treatment?

You can use the Specialist search page to find the treatment you want, from the Specialist you love. Click a specialist, choose your service, pick a time that suits you and pay using Apple Pay, Google Pay or a card.

How do I change my booking?

As long as you are not within the specialists cancellation policy, if you want to reschedule an appointment, click the appointments tab in settings and then the reschedule button.

How do I know if my booking is confirmed?

You will receive a confirmation via email or text. In the appointments page you will be able to view upcoming bookings also.

SPECIALISTS

How do I become a CUT-IT Specialist?

Download the app, sign up and complete your profile.

  • In the settings tab click ‘Become a Specialist’

  • Select your Speciality

  • Add your business details

  • Add your cancellation policy

  • Add your services

  • Save and verify your ID

The team will review your details. Once approved your app will switch to the Specialist view and you can utilise the specialist features.

Can I upgrade/downgrade my subscription?

At CUT-IT we’re all about flexibility. If you’ve taken a break from work, you can downgrade to the Free plan and keep all your reviews, pictures and clients. You can do this at any time and you’re not tied in for the year.

How do I find new clients?

Your profile will be visible to every user in the area using the CUT-IT search page. Businesses are listed based on user reviews so clients can discover and book with you easily.

CUT-IT is a powerful social media platform so building up an audience by posting to your feed is a good way to attract new customers who can come across your content from the inspiration feed.

If you are our highest priced Specialist Plan you will have access to the Bolt function. When a Bolt is used, your profile will be advertised in various high traffic spots throughout the app, making your profile more visible to clients.

Can my clients rate and review me?

Of course! Once they've completed an appointment with you, they will have the ability to leave a rating and review.

People trust people, and for this reason we recommend you encourage your customers to leave reviews and engage with you on the platform, tagging you in posts where you provided the service. Reviews are a powerful referral - let your clients be your marketing team!

You can also leave a reply to reviews which will also be visible to users of the app.

How do I use the post feature?

No more handling multiple social media accounts. Use the camera button to post a picture or video, either from the app or your photo gallery. You can tag your client in the post and if they approve, it will appear in a dedicated section of your profile page for verified posts.
