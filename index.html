FEATURES

The social app for looking good & feeling good!

Bookings and appointments

Seamlessly manage upcoming appointments, set your cancellation policy and reschedule bookings with the click of a button. An interactive calendar gives you full control over appointment management - no need to waste time entering booking information.

Cashless payments

No fumbling for cash as you can book and pay directly though the app. We offer Tipping with zero fees taken meaning you can show support to your favourite specialist.

Find the best hair & beauty specialists
