FAQs

If you have any other questions or need to contact us. Email us at support@cutitapp.co.uk

CLIENTS

Do clients need a subscription to use the app?

Clients can register on the app free of charge. They can find barbers, hairdressers, nail techs, makeup artists and more near them with no subscription. A 5% commission is applied on bookings.

How do I book a treatment?

You can use the Specialist search page to find the treatment you want, from the Specialist you love. Click a specialist, choose your service, pick a time that suits you and pay using Apple Pay, Google Pay or a card.

How do I change or cancel my booking?

As long as you are not within the specialist’s cancellation policy, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment, click the appointments tab in settings and then the reschedule button.

How do I know if my booking is confirmed?

You will receive a confirmation via email or text. In the appointments page you will be able to view upcoming bookings also.

Do you charge for bookings?

A 5% commission is applied to all bookings to help us continue investing in CUT-IT.

Can I book appointments for myself and someone else?

Yes, you can. Simply book multiple back to back appointments if you need to. Just leave a comment when booking so that your specialist knows who the appointment is for.

How can I earn discounts / rewards?

Head over to the rewards tab.You can refer a friend by sharing your unique referral code.

You’ll receive a voucher once your friend books a service on CUT-IT.You can also redeem rewards for making bookings on CUT-IT as part of our loyalty scheme.

These can be redeemed with any specialist on the app.

Do I have to download the app to book an appointment?

Yes. Currently we don’t have a web version although it is something we are considering building in the future.

Can I leave a review for my specialist?

After your service, you can view your booking history where you can leave a review and tip your specialist.

How do I know if I will get good service?

The problem with booking hair and beauty services right now, is that if it's your first time with a person, there is no telling what the quality of your treatment will be like.

CUT-IT allows you to view verified client reviews for each individual specialist, so you know that they have the skills you want and not someone else in the shop.

Each specialist has a verified post section of their profile. Here, only posts which have been approved by the client tagged in it appear. This is so you can see the specialists actual work before booking, and be sure that their feed isn’t just images from google.

Is there a fee for cancellations or missing my appointment?

If you cancel your appointment within the specialists cancellation policy, you will be charged the full amount of your service.

How do I delete my current payment method?

From the settings menu, navigate to the payments tab, click on cards and use the trash icon to delete a card on file.

SPECIALISTS

How do I become a CUT-IT Specialist?

Download the app, sign up and complete your profile.

  • In the settings tab click ‘Become a Specialist’

  • Select your Speciality

  • Add your business details

  • Add your cancellation policy

  • Add your services

  • Save and verify your ID

The team will review your details. Once approved your app will switch to the Specialist view and you can utilise the specialist features.

Can I upgrade/downgrade my subscription?

At CUT-IT we’re all about flexibility. If you’ve taken a break from work, you can downgrade to the Free plan and keep all your reviews, pictures and clients. You can do this at any time and you’re not tied in for the year.

How do I find new clients?

Your profile will be visible to every user in the area using the CUT-IT search page. Businesses are listed based on user reviews so clients can discover and book with you easily.

CUT-IT is a powerful social media platform so building up an audience by posting to your feed is a good way to attract new customers who can come across your content from the inspiration feed.

If you are our highest priced Specialist Plan you will have access to the Bolt function. When a Bolt is used, your profile will be advertised in various high traffic spots throughout the app, making your profile more visible to clients.

Can my clients rate and review me?

Of course! Once they've completed an appointment with you, they will have the ability to leave a rating and review.

People trust people, and for this reason we recommend you encourage your customers to leave reviews and engage with you on the platform, tagging you in posts where you provided the service. Reviews are a powerful referral - let your clients be your marketing team!

You can also leave a reply to reviews which will also be visible to users of the app.

How do I use the post feature?

No more handling multiple social media accounts. Use the camera button to post a picture or video, either from the app or your photo gallery. You can tag your client in the post and if they approve, it will appear in a dedicated section of your profile page for verified posts.

Do I have to confirm all appointments that get requested by clients?

Yes. This is to ensure you are available for the requested date and time.

We want CUT-IT to be a safe space for everyone to use. Making sure every specialist on the app is who they say they are adds a layer of security when using the service. All information stored is GDPR compliant and used strictly for identification purposes.

Do I receive appointment reminders?

If you have notifications enabled, you will receive notifications regarding your upcoming appointments throughout the day.

Can I contact my clients through the app?

Of course! You can message your client directly using the chat function of the CUT-IT app. They can send you pictures of styles they'd like prior to the booking and you can discuss their service before it begins!

Can I see other Specialists on the app and book appointments?

Yes. CUT-IT is a unified app for both clients and specialists. As a specialist you have access to more business management functions but can still book services and enjoy the social media features as one of your clients does.

Can I become a specialist if I only learnt my skill recently?

Most definitely! CUT-IT is here to empower everyone to master their craft and turn their hobbies into a profession. Build up an audience from the start of your career and showcase any previous work you have on your personal feed and let new clients find you.

Can I sell products through the app?

Coming soon! We aim to roll out an update which will add a products page to the specialists profile and allow users to order directly from the app.

Right now however, You can advertise any products you wish to sell by posting it to your feed and selling in person.

What if I work at multiple store locations?

Currently, we only support one business address per account. Although, you can change this anytime using the edit services tab.

Does the shop owner need to approve usage?

Currently, CUT-IT is focused on empowering each and every individual Specialist to turbo charge their business and master their craft. However, we recognise that some shops operate differently.

If your shop’s owner actively manages the shop or salon, let them know about CUT-IT to see if we’re a good fit.

How does the verified tag work?

When you make a post you can choose to tag a client or another user. If the client approves the tag request the post will appear in a different part of your profile so other users will know it's a verified post.

How do I get paid?

Payouts are handled by Stripe and money will be deposited to your bank account every 7 days.
